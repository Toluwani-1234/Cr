Fire Horse (Chinese Zodiac)

In Chinese astrology, Fire Horse refers to a year in the sexagenary cycle that occurs every 60 years. Those born in the year of the Fire Horse are believed to possess exceptional energy, passion, and an untamable spirit. The last Fire Horse year was 1966, and the next will be 2026 - the year of the Double Fire Horse convergence.

The Fire Horse is considered one of the most powerful and auspicious combinations in the Chinese zodiac, symbolizing rapid transformation, bold risk-taking, and revolutionary energy - the perfect foundation for a movement in the crypto era.
